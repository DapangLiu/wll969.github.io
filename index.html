Minds, Machines and Society Group

Prof Soroush Vosoughi leads the minds, machines and society group at Dartmouth. Our main interests lie at the intersection of machine learning, natural language processing, network science, and social media analytics. We are interested in applying these fields to study, model, and predict complex social behaviors. Currently, Our lab is working on computational tools to study privacy, propaganda, and polarization in the American public sphere.
